Ingrid Navarro

I recently graduated from Tecnologico de Monterrey in Mexico. My background is in Computer Engineering, but I am a Deep Learning and Computer Vision enthusiast. This summer, I was a Computer Vision Intern at Carbon Robotics, and before that, I was a Computer Vision intern at XLAB.

As an undergrad, I participated in the Robotics Institute Summer Scholars (RISS) program in 2017 and 2018. I joined the Navigation Laboratory where I did research on object detection systems (2017) and semantic segmentation of 3D LiDAR point clouds.

I was also part of VantTEC, an undergraduate research team that focuses on building autonomous vehicles. I particularly participated in the design of the perception system for an autonomous boat. I presented part of that project at the MICAI conference in 2018.
