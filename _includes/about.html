I'm a PhD student in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm advised by Jean Oh, head of the Bot Intelligence Group (BIG).

My research interests involve enabling autonomous navigation systems to seamlessly and safely interact with humans in shared spaces. As such, I currently work on developing algorithms and methods that assess and enhance the robustness of motion prediction models and social navigation algorithms. My ongoing works are mostly focused on the domains of aviation and autonomous driving, but I have also explored other domains such as motion in human crowds and sports.
